About Conference

ICRACS'21 is a forum for presenting excellent results and new challenges focusing in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. It brings together experts from Industry, Governments and Academicians experienced in Engineering design and Research especially in the area of Computer Science and Engineering.


New disruptive technologies bring up every day making Computer Science the most happening, dynamic and a challenging discipline. This rapid change moments all stake holders in computer science to update on daily basis to remain relevant. ICRACS'20 offer such platform for effective interaction, debate and sharing of knowledge and experience.


The ICRACS'21 being organized by Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Saveetha Engineering College offers a technical program including keynote speakers, regular presentations, workshops, academic visiting for tour. Our objective is to provide a platform which will promote and encourage researchers, scholars, academicians to carry on and accomplish their research work. We look forward for your contribution and participation to make this a successful event.
